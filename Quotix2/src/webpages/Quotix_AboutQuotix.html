About Quotix


How it Started


Quotix is a group project that was started by a team of six college students from the University of Wisconsin-Stout. We are a bunch of movie-loving people who came together and wanted to create a platform that allowed us to search up our favorite movie quotes and find information about the quote.


Updates


Currently, Quotix is in pre-alpha phase. But we are optimistic we shall have a testing/alpha phase sometime soon for our users to try out! Keep checking the site to learn when the testing phase will begin!
